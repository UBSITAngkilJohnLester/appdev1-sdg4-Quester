About Quester

Quester is a free learning platform built for IT students, aligned with UN SDG 4 — Quality Education. It provides lessons, flashcards, and quizzes to help students study anytime, anywhere.

Our Mission

SDG 4 calls for inclusive and equitable quality education for all. Quester answers that call by making IT education accessible — free, structured, and available to every student regardless of background.
